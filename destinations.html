Partner Preview Data Sources

This Partner Preview build includes live, context-only signals to demonstrate how LionGateOS Travels becomes interactive after partner approval.

  • OpenStreetMap maps (geographic context only; no listings)
  • Open‑Meteo weather reality checks (planning context)
  • Teleport city signals where available (high-level context)
  • Unsplash imagery (visual context only)
